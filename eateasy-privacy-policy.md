**Last updated: March 2026**

## 1. Introduction

EatEasy: Diet Menu Finder ("the App") is developed and operated by Northline Developments. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our App.

By using EatEasy, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Location Data
EatEasy requests access to your device's GPS location solely to find restaurants near you. Your location is:
- Only accessed when you tap "Find restaurants near me"
- Used in real time to search for nearby restaurants
- Never stored on our servers
- Never shared with third parties beyond what is required to perform the search

You can deny location permission at any time in your device settings. The App will still function without location access — you can search for restaurants manually.

### 2.2 Search Queries
When you search for a restaurant, your search query (restaurant name and city) is sent to our secure backend server to perform the analysis. This data is:
- Not linked to your identity
- Not stored permanently
- Used only to generate your requested menu analysis

### 2.3 Device Information
We do not collect device identifiers, advertising IDs, or any personal device information.

### 2.4 Personal Information
EatEasy does not require you to create an account. We do not collect your name, email address, phone number, or any other personal information.

---

## 3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information described above only to:
- Provide restaurant search results based on your location
- Analyse restaurant menus for your selected diet
- Improve the accuracy and performance of the App

We do not use your information for advertising, profiling, or any other purpose.

---

## 4. Third-Party Services

EatEasy uses the following third-party services to operate:

### Anthropic (Claude AI)
Restaurant menu analysis is powered by Anthropic's Claude AI. Search queries are sent to Anthropic's API to generate dietary analysis. Anthropic's privacy policy can be found at: https://www.anthropic.com/privacy

### Google Places API
Nearby restaurant search is powered by Google Places API. Your approximate GPS coordinates are sent to Google to return nearby restaurant results. Google's privacy policy can be found at: https://policies.google.com/privacy

### Render
Our backend server is hosted on Render. No personal data is stored on these servers. Render's privacy policy can be found at: https://render.com/privacy

---

## 5. Data Storage and Security

EatEasy does not store any personal data. All queries are processed in real time and discarded. We use HTTPS encryption for all communications between the App and our backend server.
